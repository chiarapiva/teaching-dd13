Description

In conclusion, we wanted to analyze the policies of the different social media concerning the topic of hate speech and free speech in order to understand the degree of attention they devote to this topic. To do this, we looked for the previously selected hate terms used by Alt-Right on the social media they most commonly use and analyzed them quantitatively. To understand how much hate speech is spread on each platform in relation to its number of users, we have collected data on the average number of users per social network.

Finally, in order to understand the degree of attention and deepening devoted by each social media to the topic of hate speeches, we collected the texts of their policies related to this topic and counted the number of words. The graph shows a proportional relationship between the number of words used in different social media policies and the degree of attention paid to hate speeches, as well as the number of monthly users. In conclusion, we can say that the greater the number of users on a given social media, the greater the number of hate words present, but above all we can see that there is an increase in the number of words used in the policies that regulate hate speeches, and therefore a greater attention devoted to the topic.
